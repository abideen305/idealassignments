Descriptive Statistics

Descriptive statistics is a short descriptive coefficient that summarizes the data set, which can be either a team of the entire or a sample of the population. It is the measure of central tendencies and variable measures. We provide statistics assignment help on all type of descriptive statistics including central leaning of ungrouped data, measures of mean, dispersion, variance & standard deviations of ungrouped and grouped data, bids of those measures, measures of position, box, and millimeter plot, etc.

INFERENTIAL STATISTICS

Inferential statistics is a set of assumptions that are made on the basis of information found by Descriptive Statistics. This type of statistics is used to take a random sample of data taken from a population to describe and make deductions about that population. We provide statistics assignment help on all the type of inferential statistics including Hypothesis Tests about the Mean and Proportion, Estimation and Hypothesis Testing Two Populations, Estimation of Mean and Proportion, Analysis of Variance (ANOVA), Analysis of covariance (ANCOVA), Statistical significance (t-test, z-test), Correlation and Regression, Confidence interval, Chi-square and lot more.
